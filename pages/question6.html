Kanna

Maple Story

In MapleStory, Kanna is a unique Magician class from the Sengoku job branch, originating from the Japanese-inspired region of Momijigaoka. She wields a fan as her main weapon and has a familiar (Haku) who supports her in battle.

Kanna's skills focus heavily on crowd control, summoning spirits, and using Shikigami abilities to deal damage over wide areas, making her one of the best classes for mobbing. Her playstyle includes passive buffs that increase spawn rates and yield higher EXP, which makes her highly desirable for farming. Kanna also excels at supportive abilities like providing party members with extra mana and health regeneration. However, her reliance on mana and specific skill combos can make her more challenging to manage in long battles.

Buccaneers are known for their high mobility, impressive burst damage, and transformation skills, which allow them to unleash powerful combos with a fluid, high-speed combat style.

Connoneer

Maple Story

In MapleStory, the Cannoneer is a Pirate class from the Explorer branch, known for its explosive, high-impact attacks and playful combat style. Cannoneers wield a hand cannon, which grants them long-range capabilities, and they partner with a companion monkey who supports in battle. This class excels at dealing damage across wide areas, making them particularly strong for mobbing and handling large groups of enemies.

A distinctive aspect of the Cannoneer is its synergy with a loyal monkey companion, who aids in combat and offers supportive buffs. The Monkey Magic buff boosts essential stats, including HP, MP, and attack power, enhancing both the Cannoneer and any nearby party members, which makes the Cannoneer highly valued in group settings. For added survivability, Cannoneers also have Barrel Roulette, which can randomly grant helpful effects like increased attack speed or additional damage reduction.

With its combination of powerful AoE skills, self-sustaining abilities, and supportive party buffs, the Cannoneer is an all-around powerhouse that’s fun and accessible for beginners yet capable of high-level performance in both solo and team play. The Cannoneer’s gameplay is energetic and explosive, perfect for players who enjoy big, satisfying hits and a well-rounded combat experience.
